    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I create a new bill?

    Go to Create Bill in the sidebar. Enter the customer name and phone number, add products and quantities, then click Save and Print to save the invoice and download it as a PDF.

    How do I add a new product?

    Navigate to Products in the Admin Panel. Click Add Product, fill in the product name, price, category and stock quantity, then save.

    How do I download an invoice as PDF?

    You can download a PDF in two ways: (1) After creating a bill, click Save and Print — the invoice PDF downloads automatically. (2) From the Dashboard, find the invoice in the Recent Transactions table and click Download PDF next to it.

    How do I change my profile name or photo?

    Click your profile icon in the top-right corner, then select Edit Profile. Update your display name or upload a new photo, then click Save Changes.
